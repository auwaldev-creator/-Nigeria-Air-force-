Game da Wannan Farfajiya

Wannan samfuri ne na yadda shafin daukar aiki na NAF zai iya kasancewa a tsarin zamani. Ainihin shafin na bukatar "backend" mai karfi don tabbatar da bayanai, adana takardu, da tsaro.

Yadda Zaka Cika Form

  1. Kirkiri Account: Yi rijista da ingantaccen email da password (a `signup.html`).
  2. Shiga Ciki: Yi amfani da bayanan da kayi rijista da su ka shiga (a `login.html`).
  3. Cika Bayanai: Cika dukkan bayanan da ake bukata a shafin cike form (`apply.html`).
  4. Adana Bayanai: Wannan demo yana adana bayanan a browser (localStorage) ne kawai.
